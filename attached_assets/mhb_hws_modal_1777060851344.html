What affects your score

HVAC, roof, plumbing, electrical, and water heater all lose points annually based on expected lifespan. Older systems cost you more.
Tasks past their due date subtract points daily. The longer they sit unresolved, the steeper the penalty.
Completing scheduled maintenance recovers points and builds a verified track record for buyers and insurers.
Logging verified work — by a pro or yourself — protects your score from depreciation and builds your home's history.
Replacing an aging system resets its depreciation clock and delivers a significant one-time score boost.
Unknown system ages are penalized until documented. Complete records across all systems score significantly higher.
